About

Our group focuses on particle detection and data analysis on experimental high energy physics (hep-ex & nucl-ex). We aim to research fundamental structure of particles and interaction by developing high performance tools and methods.

Fields : QGP, Particle Detection, Cosmic-ray Experiment

Collaborations : RHIC-STAR, BEPC-BESIII, FAIR-CBM, LHC-ALICE

Detectors : MRPC, SiPM, PMT, GEM, Scintillator
